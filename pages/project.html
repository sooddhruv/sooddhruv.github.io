× Image Steganography [2022]
In my image steganography project, I utilized CNN to hide one picture inside another, making it invisible to the human eye. Unlike traditional methods, our approach used neural networks to identify optimal locations for concealing information in the cover image, enhancing security. A decoder network was trained to extract the hidden picture from the modified cover image. The modifications to the container image were imperceptible, ensuring data confidentiality even if the original picture was unavailable. The project showcased a robust and secure method for image steganography, applicable in data privacy and secure communication scenarios.

× Predicting Trends in Ethical Consumerism [2024]
In this project, I built a Python pipeline to analyze public sentiment on corporate sustainability by scraping Reddit discussions using PRAW and applying sentiment analysis tools like VADER and TextBlob. The goal was to identify trends in ethical consumerism by aligning sentiment scores with stock price movements via the yfinance API. This enabled the detection of sentiment-driven patterns in stock performance, helping to explore the financial impact of ESG-related public opinion.
